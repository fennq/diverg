Q2 roadmap

Blockchain & investigations

With our latest investigation wrapped, we are doubling down on the blockchain side of Diverg over the coming weeks—sharpening on-chain security and turning real-world case experience into a stronger product.

  • On-chain investigation — Expand how we track wallet activity, trace transactions, and surface meaningful insights faster and more clearly across our tools.
  • Forensics — Refine tooling from what we have learned in the field so we can go deeper, move faster, and deliver more actionable intelligence.
  • Detection & reliability — Improve suspicious-behaviour signals, data aggregation, and the robustness of our blockchain analysis engine so risks surface earlier and outputs are easier to trust.
  • Private platform — Keep advancing the private product with more advanced blockchain-focused capabilities for users who need deeper analysis and higher-level workflows.

This phase is about focus: stronger on-chain investigations, clearer outputs, and a product shaped by hands-on work on serious cases.

Public vs private. Our public tools are for broad use and early access—they are not the same depth as full forensic run-downs such as our Drift case work, which is aimed at our private platform and professional investigations.
